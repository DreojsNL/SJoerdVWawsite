SWR

In my initial development phase, I've created a racing game that not only reflects my passion for game development but also my deep appreciation for nostalgic PlayStation 1 rally games, such as those by Collin McRae. My creation, SWR (Stylized World Rally), is a tribute to the iconic PS1 style, featuring a carefully chosen art style that captures the charm of that bygone era of gaming.
